Who We Are

Coffee Corner Stop was created to provide delicious fresh food in an independent environment in the center of Byfleet. Enticing customers with perfect coffee, wholesome & nutritious sandwiches, homemade juices & tempting treats.

Everything is either handmade by us or by carefully selected suppliers, who share a passion for all things delicious, to ensure the very best quality & taste.
